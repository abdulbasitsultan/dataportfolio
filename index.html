How I Deliver BI Work

From business question to live reporting product.

I work across the full BI lifecycle, from clarifying the business question and shaping the data foundation to building, publishing, maintaining and improving reporting products after delivery.

01

Understand the reporting problem

Clarify the business question, KPI logic, audience and decisions the report needs to support.

02

Prepare the data foundation

Work with teams and data owners to understand sources, identify gaps and agree the reporting process.

03

Build the reporting model

Create data models, DAX measures, validation checks and dashboard views that make performance easier to read.

04

Validate the numbers

Check outputs against source data, investigate inconsistencies and review logic with stakeholders.

05

Publish and manage Power BI Apps

Package dashboards into Power BI Apps, manage audience access and organise report navigation.

06

Maintain and improve after delivery

Monitor refresh schedules, resolve data issues, document changes and improve reports as needs evolve.
